Company-overview

Our company has accumulated 15 years of experience in the development and sales of spare-parts for beverage-filling machines.

The components are manufactured according European industry-standards using selected materials and technologies, to ensure the quality and reliabilty of the parts.

Our team is located in Europe as well as in Asia, to ensure, that we can work close with our customers and fulfill all their requirements in the best possible way.
